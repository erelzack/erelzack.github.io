\section{Why this works}

This post is written as a simple \LaTeX{} file.
The website loads the file and converts common \LaTeX{} structure into HTML.
Mathematics is left in \LaTeX{} form and rendered by MathJax.

\section{A small equation}

For example, inline math like \(E = mc^2\) works, and display math works too:

\[
    \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
\]

Standard equation environments also work:

\begin{equation}
    \nabla \cdot \vec{E} = \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0}
\end{equation}
